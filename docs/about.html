Pablo Polledri is an independent filmmaker specializing in animation and illustration, with an internationally recognized career.
His work, characterized by a personal style and a critical perspective, ranges from auteur short films to commissioned projects for cinema, television, digital platforms, mapping, and installations.
For over 15 years, he has directed and produced animated projects comprehensively, taking on roles in scriptwriting, animation, directing, post-production, as well as music and sound design. His shorts have been showcased at festivals across five continents, earning awards at prestigious events such as the Goya Awards (Spain), FILMETS, Fantoche, AniMate (Australia), among many others.
In addition to his personal work, he collaborates as a freelancer with studios, brands, and organizations on animated pieces, motion graphics, and illustrations. His approach blends analog and digital tools, with a strong commitment to visual storytelling.
